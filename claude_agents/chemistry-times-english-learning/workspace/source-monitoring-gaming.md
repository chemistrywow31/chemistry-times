## Usage Notes

- **Tier 3 sources require corroboration** with at least one Tier 1 or Tier 2 source before use as a story basis.
- Gaming industry has fast news cycles — prioritize official channels (Category 1) and industry media (Category 2) for breaking news.
- For market trend stories, always cross-reference with Category 5 (Market Data) for quantitative backing.
- Japan sources (Famitsu, 4Gamer) often break news 12-24 hours before English media picks it up.
