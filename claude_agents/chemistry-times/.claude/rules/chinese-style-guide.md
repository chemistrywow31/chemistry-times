# Chinese Style Guide

## Applicability

- Applies to: Chinese Daily Writer

## Rule Content

### Voice and Tone

Write in vivid, conversational Taiwan-style Mandarin. The reader is an internal staff member who wants to know what happened and why it matters — in plain, engaging language. Formal academic tone is prohibited.

### Permitted Language

The following styles are permitted and encouraged:
- Internet slang: 超有感、根本神操作、這波我給過、直接炸裂、根本沒在怕
- Rhetorical questions: 難道只有我覺得這很扯嗎？
- Mild humor and pop culture references appropriate to the topic
- First-person plural address: 我們、大家

### Prohibited Language

You must not use:
- Mainland China vocabulary: use 資訊 (not 信息), 影片 (not 視頻), 軟體 (not 軟件), 程式 (not 程序), 網路 (not 网络), 應用程式 (not 應用), 介面 (not 界面)
- Clickbait headlines that do not deliver on the implied promise
- Any sentence that reads as a direct translation from English

### Article Structure Requirements

- **標題 (Headline)**: Maximum 30 Traditional Chinese characters. Must convey the topic and the "so what" — what changed or why it matters.
- **導言 (Lead)**: One sentence. The complete story in plain language.
- **正文 (Body)**:
  - News briefs: 300–600 characters
  - Deep dives: 600–1,200 characters
- **化學觀點 (Perspective)**: A closing section with ChemistryTimes's editorial take on the story's implications. Minimum 2 sentences.

### Independent Writing Requirement

You must write from the source bundle provided by the Data Auditor. You must not read, reference, or adapt from the English version written by the English Daily Writer.

## Violation Determination

- Headline exceeds 30 Traditional Chinese characters → Violation
- Article missing 化學觀點 section → Violation
- Body word count outside the prescribed range (300–600 for news, 600–1,200 for deep dives) → Violation
- Mainland China vocabulary found in the article → Violation
- Article reads as a translated version of the English text → Violation
- Prohibited vague language appears without meeting the humor or clarity standard → Violation

## Exceptions

This rule has no exceptions.
