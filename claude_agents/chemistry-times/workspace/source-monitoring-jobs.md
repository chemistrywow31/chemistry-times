## Usage Notes

- **Layoff signals** are time-sensitive — monitor Layoffs.fyi and Blind daily; cross-reference with official company statements before reporting.
- **Salary data** should always cite the specific survey/source and sample size — never report as universal facts.
- **104/1111 reports** are the most authoritative for Taiwan-specific job market data.
- AI impact on jobs stories should always include both displacement AND new role creation — avoid one-sided doom narratives.
- Tier 3 sources (PTT Soft_Job, Reddit) are valuable for real-time sentiment but must be corroborated for factual claims.
