## Usage Notes

- **Funding round data**: Always cite the source database (Crunchbase, PitchBook) and note if amounts are confirmed or estimated.
- **IPO coverage**: Use Renaissance Capital and Dealogic as primary; media coverage as supplementary.
- **Asia deals**: DealStreetAsia and Tech in Asia are the most reliable for SEA funding data; 36Kr and IT 桔子 for China.
- **AI funding** is a cross-cutting theme — check Category 5 sources alongside general VC media when covering AI investment stories.
- For Taiwan capital market stories, always cross-reference 鉅亨網 / 工商時報 / 經濟日報 for local market context.
- Tier 3 (Reddit, PTT Stock) sources are sentiment indicators only — never report funding amounts from social media without official confirmation.
